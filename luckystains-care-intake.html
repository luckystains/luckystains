Availability
A note on openings

luckystains care operates with limited independent openings. I work full-time as a contracted pet care specialist with VIPet Sitter Services — a professional, insured service with its own team and service area. If I'm unavailable or bookings are closed, I'm happy to connect you with vetted sitters I trust.

Independent slots are offered on a first-come, first-served basis. Submitting the intake form holds your place in the queue — confirmation follows once I've reviewed your information.
